---how can I play?---

Move monster to the left by click 'Left' button, press 'A' on keyboard, or press left arrow on keyboard
Move monster to the right by click 'Right' button, press 'D' on keyboard, or press right arrow on keyboard
Drop the monster by click 'Hit' button, or press down arrow on keyboard

Hit the green for 100 points | Hit the purple for 200 points | Hit the red and you'll get punishment
You only have 5 lifes
